Greek Salad

Being half Greek, I have come to love the easy, delicious meal of a Greek Salad! It is healthy and scrumptious.
The beauty of a Greek Salad, is you can add or take out any ingredient!
Here is my quick salad recipe! I did not add quantities, since each ingredient is based on personal preference.
I included some nice Greek music to enjoy while cooking!

Ingredients

  • Cucumber
  • Cherry Tomatoes
  • Red Onion
  • Kalamata Olives
  • Feta Cheese
  • Lemon Juice
  • Red Wine Vinegar
  • Olive Oil
  • Oregano
  • Salt and Pepper

    • Directions

    1. Chop vegetables into cubes
    2. Add seasoning to taste
    3. Add olive oil, red wine vinegar, and lemon juice to taste
